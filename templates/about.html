About Our Sentiment Analysis App

Uncover emotions behind online discussions using AI.

What is This App?

Our sentiment analysis tool scans Reddit discussions and analyzes emotions in real-time, helping users track public opinion on various topics.

Purpose of This Project

Understanding sentiment is crucial in decision-making. This tool enables businesses, researchers, and individuals to track opinions, predict trends, and understand emotions in online discussions.

How It Works

1. Extracts Data

Pulls comments from Reddit discussions.

2. Analyzes Emotions

Uses AI to classify sentiments as Positive, Negative, or Neutral.

3. Generates Insights

Summarizes overall sentiment trends.

4. Visualizes Data

Displays easy-to-understand charts and statistics.

Who Can Benefit From It?

  • Businesses: Track brand reputation and customer opinions.
  • Researchers: Study social trends and public opinion.
  • Marketers: Understand consumer sentiment for better campaigns.
  • General Users: Stay updated on trending topics.

Research on Sentiment Analysis

Good knowledge, attitudes, and practices (KAP) towards obstetric danger signs lead to fast health-seeking behavior, protecting both mother and child. A lack of awareness about these signs can cause delays in seeking care, contributing to maternal mortality.

This research aims to increase awareness of obstetric danger signs among pregnant mothers at Kitui County Referral Hospital. It is a descriptive cross-sectional study involving 80 respondents selected through random sampling. Data will be collected via questionnaires, processed using SPSS version 26, and presented using tables, graphs, and charts.
